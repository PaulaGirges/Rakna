FIND PARKING NEAR YOU

The free Parking.com mobile app makes your on-the-go
parking experience that much simpler. Find the best parking
spot near your destination in convenient and safe parking
garages and lots across the United States.

How Our Parking App Works

When downloading the Parking.com app, search by entering an
address, landmark, or neighborhood to see available parking in
that area. When you reserve and pre-pay, you’ll guarantee your
parking spot. Plus, you can download mobile coupons directly
through the app for discounts at select parking locations.

Features of the Free Parking.com App:

✓ Compare rates
✓ Receive turn-by-turn directions to the garage or lot
✓ Send a text message to eligible locations so attendants know when you’ll be picking up your vehicle
✓ Receive in-app alerts
✓ In-app reminders help you never forget where you parked
✓ Bookmark for faster bookings
✓ Download coupons & pay in advance to park

Don’t let parking in a city or at the airport be a hassle. Let our
app help you find parking when you need the perfect spot in
your neighborhood. With Parking.com, just park and go. Quick,
convenient, and at the tip of your finger to make life easier.
